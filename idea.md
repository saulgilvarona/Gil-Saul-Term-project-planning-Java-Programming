Project idea: An supermarket administrator:

The implementation can have the following ones:

- Place an order of food/other goods, updating the existing quantities in the storage.
- Once that the order is done, the money will also update.
- Create a bill when someone buys something, the date where the customer has bought the goods will be shown, then you can also check the money made and the name of the customers that bought something that day.
- It will also update the goods that the customer has bought, adding money to the account and also the employee that attended the person.
- Adding/removing employees.
- Creating different categories for employees (regular, manager).

Personally I think this is a really interesting idea that can be expanded or contracted depending on the time remaining. Its also nothing crazy complicated that we haven't being taught and its also a good way of implementing code in real life scenario.
